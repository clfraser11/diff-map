LEGEND

The world has changed. It is ever changing.  We need a different MAP.

 

K(no)wmap.  We know our destination but acknowledge that we are lost. (LRS) 

Do we? (CF)

 

Many of us are on a different MAP, not quite in synch with the contexts in which we find ourselves.  Take innovators and interdisciplinary thinkers, those who come from somewhere else, those who are going there, perhaps all of us in our different ways.  We must chart a new course—create new ground on which to stand.
